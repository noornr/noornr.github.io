What is
Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a secure digital system for storing information. Instead of keeping data in one place, it is shared across many computers around the world.

How Does Blockchain Work?

Think of blockchain like a digital notebook.

📄

1. Block

Every page in the notebook is called a Block.

➜
💾

2. Store Information

Each block stores transactions or important records.

➜
🔗

3. Chain

Every block connects to the previous block, creating a Blockchain.

Why is Blockchain Special?

Blockchain is trusted because it is secure, transparent, and decentralized.

🔒

Secure

Once information is stored on the blockchain, it is extremely difficult to change or delete.

🌍

Decentralized

No single company or government controls the blockchain. Thousands of computers maintain it.

👀

Transparent

Public blockchains allow anyone to verify transactions, increasing trust.

⚡

Fast & Reliable

Digital assets can be transferred globally without relying on traditional banks.
